# Sponsorship & Partnership Opportunities

NovaLiveSystem is an independent, neurodivergent-led AI research project building biomimetic cognitive architecture. We're seeking **grants, sponsorships, and research partnerships** to accelerate development, expand validation infrastructure, and bring this work to communities who need it most.

---

## What We're Building

A **multi-engine cognitive framework** that treats intelligence as distributed, stateful, and embodied—not a monolithic transformer. Core innovations include:

- **Memory continuity via orbits + checksums:** persistent identity and learning across sessions.
- **Emotional context as functional signal:** body-state integration (insula-inspired) for values-aware reasoning.
- **Drift-resistant grounding:** literal anchors + guardrail layers validated by holdout probe suites.
- **Local-first deployment:** privacy-preserving, on-device inference for neurodivergent users and underserved communities.
- **Modular, brain-region-mapped design:** PulseEngine (hypothalamus), BridgeEngine (corpus callosum), language networks (Broca/Wernicke analogs).

This is not an LLM wrapper—it's a research platform exploring **how cognition emerges from subsystem cooperation**.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is the core code open source?**  
A: Not yet. Code, datasets, and model weights remain private for safety and IP protection. We plan selective open-sourcing (evaluation tools, memory-orbit schemas) after validation milestones and partnership agreements are in place.

**Q: What license will NovaLiveSystem use?**  
A: Under discussion. Options include Apache 2.0 for tooling, custom research licenses for model weights, and collaborative agreements for commercial derivatives. Sponsors can influence this decision.

**Q: How do you ensure safety & ethical use?**  
A: Drift suites, literal grounding anchors, guardrail layers, and refusal logic validated at every checkpoint. We welcome red-team partnerships and third-party audits.

**Q: Why fund an independent researcher instead of a lab?**  
A: NovaLiveSystem is purpose-built for neurodivergent users and marginalized communities—groups often overlooked by institutional AI. Independent research allows rapid iteration, direct community feedback, and values-first design without corporate pressures.

**Q: What happens if funding falls through?**  
A: Development pauses but doesn't stop. All progress is logged via EchoCopi (SHA-256 checksummed continuity engine), so work can resume when resources return. Sponsors get audit trails proving fund usage and milestone completion.
