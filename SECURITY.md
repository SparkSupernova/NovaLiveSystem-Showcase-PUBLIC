# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security concern related to NovaLiveSystem—whether in documentation, conceptual design, or any public artifact—please report it privately.

**Email:** hello.many@sparkpluggedts.com  
**Subject Line:** `[SECURITY] NovaLiveSystem — Brief Description`

---

## What to Report

- Potential safety issues in documented AI behavior
- Privacy concerns in public artifacts (screenshots, logs, etc.)
- Vulnerabilities in conceptual architecture that could be exploited
- Anything that seems "off" from a safety or ethics perspective

---

## What Happens Next

1. **Acknowledgment** — You'll receive a response within 72 hours
2. **Assessment** — We'll evaluate the concern and its impact
3. **Resolution** — If valid, we'll address it and credit you (if desired)
4. **Disclosure** — We'll coordinate any public disclosure with you

---

## Scope

This policy covers:
- This public showcase repository
- Any documentation, images, or artifacts published here
- Conceptual architecture descriptions

This policy does **not** cover:
- The private NovaLiveSystem codebase (not publicly accessible)
- Third-party dependencies or platforms (GitHub, etc.)

---

## Safe Harbor

We will not pursue legal action against anyone who:
- Reports vulnerabilities in good faith
- Avoids privacy violations, data destruction, or service disruption
- Gives us reasonable time to respond before public disclosure
